Tracy Catherine Nalubwama - Website Site Plan

Site Name

Local History

This name reflects the website's focus on educating the community about its historical significance and notable landmarks.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for local history enthusiasts, providing information on historical sites, landmark profiles, visitor information, and oral histories from community members.

Scenarios

  • What are the significant historical landmarks in my area?
  • How can I learn more about local history events and tours?
